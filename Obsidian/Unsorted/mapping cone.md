# Chain Complexes

For $f\in \mathsf{Ch}_{{\mathsf{R}{\hbox{-}}\mathsf{Mod}}}(A, B)$ a morphism of chain complex of $R{\hbox{-}}$modules, the **mapping cone** complex is `
<span class="math display">
\begin{align*}
\operatorname{cone}(f) := A[1] \oplus B, 
\quad d =
\begin{bmatrix}
d_A & 0 
\\
f & d_B
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}
<span>`{=html}
