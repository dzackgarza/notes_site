- The Yoneda embedding: the functional functor:
$$
Y: \cat{C} \to \Fun(\cat C, \Set) = \Presh_\Set(\cat C)  \quad
Y(\wait) := \Fun(\wait, \Set) \circ (\wait)\op \quad
X \mapsto \Hom_{\cat{C} } (\wait, X)
$$
